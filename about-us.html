Our Story 01

Your Vision — Our Finest Execution

United Elite Group was founded in 2012 with a single conviction: that New York City homeowners deserved a luxury renovation contractor who treated their home with the same care and precision they would expect from any bespoke service.

Over thirteen years, we've grown into a team of 42 high-end specialists and completed more than 150 projects across Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens — but that founding conviction has never changed. Every project, regardless of scale, receives our full attention, expertise, and commitment to excellence.

Whether you're a homeowner with a clear vision, an interior designer seeking a reliable execution partner, or an architect who needs a contractor who truly understands your drawings — United Elite Group is built to exceed your expectations at every step.

How We Work 04

Partners from the Very Beginning

01

Planning & Estimates

We begin with a thorough walkthrough of your space, review of any existing drawings, and a deep conversation about your goals, timeline, and budget. We then produce a detailed scope of work, material specifications, and a fully itemized estimate — so you know exactly what you're getting before we begin.

02

Construction Process

Once plans are approved and contracts signed, our project manager takes the reins. Every trade is sequenced precisely, materials are ordered and staged, and a dedicated superintendent oversees the site daily. You receive weekly written updates and have direct access to your project manager throughout.

03

Protocol & Safety

We handle all DOB permit filings, co-op alteration agreement compliance, and building management coordination. Our team adheres strictly to work-hour regulations and building rules — protecting your relationship with your building while keeping the project on track. Eco-friendly waste management and dust containment are standard.

04

Service & Guarantee

Before we hand over the keys, we complete a detailed punch-list walkthrough with you — addressing every item to your satisfaction. We provide a written workmanship warranty and remain available post-completion for any maintenance questions or follow-up needs. The relationship doesn't end when construction does.
