Renovation 03

Renovation Services

From full gut renovations to targeted room transformations — every project receives the same uncompromising attention to quality and detail.

Full Gut Renovation

Comprehensive top-to-bottom interior overhaul, from demolition through all trades, custom finishes, and final inspections. We manage it all.

Kitchen Renovation

Custom kitchen design and construction — bespoke cabinetry, stone countertops, premium appliances, plumbing, and electrical from a single team.

Bathroom Renovation

Spa-inspired transformations — heated floors, steam showers, wet rooms, handcrafted tile work, and fixtures sourced from the finest suppliers.

Full Townhouse

Multi-floor townhouse renovations managed as a single project — from basement to rooftop, with precise sequencing across all trades.

Basement Renovation

Below-grade transformations: waterproofing, egress windows, mechanical systems, and full residential fit-out to create genuinely livable space.

Exterior Terrace Design

Rooftop and terrace build-outs — structural waterproofing, composite or hardwood decking, planters, drainage, and outdoor kitchen integration.

Construction 04

Construction Services

All trades performed in-house by our licensed, vetted specialists — no subcontractor handoffs, no accountability gaps.

Custom Millwork

Built-ins, cabinetry, wall paneling, coffered ceilings, and custom furniture — designed and fabricated to your exact specifications.

Plumbing

Complete residential plumbing: supply, drain, waste, vent, fixture installation, water heater, radiant floor, and steam systems.

Electrical

Full electrical systems — service upgrades, panel work, lighting circuits, smart home integration, EV charging, and low-voltage systems.

Flooring

Installation and refinishing of wide-plank hardwood, engineered wood, marble, stone, and large-format tile — including radiant-heat systems.

Drywall & Plaster

Smooth-finish drywall, Level 5 finish, ornamental plasterwork, curved walls, and restoration of historic plaster detail in landmark buildings.

Painting

Interior finish painting with premium materials — walls, ceilings, trim, millwork — with meticulous surface preparation and clean results.

Structural

Load-bearing wall removal, beam installation, floor joist work, and structural reinforcement — fully engineered and DOB-permitted.

Design & Planning 05

Design & Planning

We offer pre-construction design support to ensure every decision is made with the end result — and the budget — in mind.

Project Planning

Comprehensive scope development, scheduling, budget alignment, and pre-construction coordination with your architect, designer, and building management. We set the project up for success before demolition begins.

Design Consultation

Material selection guidance, space planning review, fixture and finish sourcing, and aesthetic direction from our senior design team — ensuring your vision is buildable, beautiful, and on-budget.
