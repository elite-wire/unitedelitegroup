Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your renovation journey with us.

Get in Touch

We specialize in luxury residential renovations across New York City — including full gut renovations, kitchen and bathroom remodels, full townhouse projects, basement conversions, and exterior terrace design. We work in condos, co-ops, and townhomes throughout Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens.

Quality is embedded at every stage. We use only licensed, vetted tradespeople, conduct regular site inspections, and have a dedicated project manager on every job. We also perform pre-close quality walkthroughs before any trade covers another trade's work.

We handle all DOB filings, co-op alteration agreement compliance, and condominium board approvals in-house. Our team is deeply familiar with NYC's permitting process, including landmark requirements and HPD regulations, so your project stays on track without bureaucratic delays.

Timelines vary by scope. A bathroom renovation typically runs 4–6 weeks; a kitchen 6–10 weeks; a full gut renovation 4–7 months. We provide a detailed project schedule at the start of every engagement and update it weekly so you always know where things stand.
