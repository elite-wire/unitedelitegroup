About the Partnership 01

Your Vision, Our Execution

For architects and interior designers working in New York City, the difference between an extraordinary project and a disappointing one often comes down to the contractor. We built United Elite Group to be that contractor — reliable, skilled, and fully aligned with your design intent.

We don't just build to spec. We read drawings carefully, ask the right questions early, flag constructability issues before they become change orders, and execute with the level of craft your clients expect and deserve.

Whether you're an independent designer with one project, a firm with ongoing work, or an architect managing a complex landmark restoration — we are the execution partner you've been looking for.
